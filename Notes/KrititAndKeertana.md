


Tyāgarāja introduced the structure for a Kriti by a pallavi, followed by Anu-pallvai and then the charanams to reinforce the theme that was set in the pallavi. He also introduced the embellishments (sangatīs) for the first time. Though the Kriti and Keertana follow a similar format in sāhityam, the emphasis is what makes the difference. While the sangatīs play the major role in the Kriti, the sāhityam plays the main role in the Keertana, like in the Divyanāma Sankeertana or Utsava Sampradaya Keertana.
